Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Introduction

MONU Planner ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account
  • Calendar Data: Events and calendar information when you connect Apple Calendar or Google Calendar
  • App Usage Data: Tasks, goals, habits, and other planning data you create within the app

Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and crash reports
  • Notification preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the MONU Planner service
  • Sync your calendar data across devices
  • Send notifications and reminders
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your planning data (tasks, goals, habits) is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage methods. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync with cloud services.

Cloud Integration

When you connect Apple Calendar or Google Calendar:

  • Calendar data is accessed through official APIs
  • We do not store your calendar data on our servers
  • All calendar operations happen directly between your device and the respective service

Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Authentication is handled securely through Apple and Google's official systems
  • We implement user isolation to ensure data privacy between different users

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

The only third-party services we integrate with are:

  • Apple Calendar: For calendar synchronization (when you choose to connect)
  • Google Calendar: For calendar synchronization (when you choose to connect)
  • Apple Push Notification Service: For sending local notifications

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Control: Choose which calendar services to connect
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and calendar sync at any time

Data Retention

Your data is retained as follows:

  • App Data: Stored locally on your device until you delete the app or your account
  • Account Information: Stored until you delete your account
  • Calendar Data: Not stored by us - only accessed through official APIs

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
